Set-OutlookSignatures, the Benefactor Circle add-on and the Outlook add-in are now available in **version 4.29.0**. This update introduces critical security continuity measures, enhanced compliance documentation, and several internal robustness fixes to keep your deployments running smoothly.

### Code-Signing Certificate Update (Annual Rotation)

Thanks to a generous donation from our partner **[ExplicIT Consulting](https://explicitconsulting.at)**, our Enterprise Validated (EV) code-signing certificate has been renewed.

- **Action Required:** If your endpoint security software (such as AppLocker, Microsoft Defender, or CrowdStrike) is strictly locked to our previous certificate, please update your configuration rules to accept the new one.
- **Future Changes:** To comply with updated industry-wide Certificate Authority (CA) guidelines, this certificate will now be rotated annually.
- **Better Appending:** The update process now appends ExplicIT’s certificate instead of replacing existing ones, ensuring original certificates from Microsoft or the .NET Foundation remain fully intact.

### New Security Questionnaire & Resources

To streamline internal IT and compliance reviews, we have added a dedicated **[Security Questionnaire](https://set-outlooksignatures.com/details#security-questionnaire)** section to our documentation. It provides clear answers to standard security and data privacy questions.

Additionally, we’ve included links to our latest deep-dive articles:

- _Simulation Mode:_ [Goodbye Rollout Panic: How to Test Outlook Signatures & Out-of-Office Notes Without Accessing Live Mailboxes](https://set-outlooksignatures.com/blog/2026/05/19/simulation-mode)
- _Summer Campaigns:_ [Hot Tips for Cool Signatures: How to Make Your Summer Campaigns Shine](https://set-outlooksignatures.com/blog/2026/06/02/hot-tips)

### Key Robustness & Fixes

- **Assembly Resolver Update:** Resolved an issue where DLL files downloaded from the web with the "Mark of the Web" restriction could not be loaded.
- **OutlookSignatureName priorization:** Fixed the `OutlookSignatureName` behavior in the [INI configuration](https://set-outlooksignatures.com/details#signature-and-oof-template-file-format) to prevent overwriting signature names already assigned to higher-priority mailboxes.
- **Dependency Upgrades:** Bumped underlying libraries including MSAL.Net, libphonenumber-csharp, and msal-browser.
